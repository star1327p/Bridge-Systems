Blackwood

Blackwood 4NT is used to ask for aces. Responses show the number of aces by steps. 5NT is then used to ask for kings; 5NT guarantees the partnership holds all four aces.

4NT Ace Asking:

  • 5♣: 0 or 4 aces

  • 5♦: 1 ace

  • 5♥: 2 aces

  • 5♠: 3 aces

5NT King Asking:

  • 6♣: 0 or 4 kings

  • 6♦: 1 king

  • 6♥: 2 kings

  • 6♠: 3 kings
