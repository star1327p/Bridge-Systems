Gerber

4♣ is Gerber over any 1NT OR 2NT by partner, including a rebid of 1NT OR 2NT. Responses show the number of aces, by steps, just as over a Blackwood 4NT. 5♣ is used to ask for kings.

Ace Asking:

1NT — 4♣

  • 4♦: 0 or 3 aces

  • 4♥: 1 or 4 aces

  • 4♠: 2 aces, bad hand

  • 4NT: 2 aces, good hand

If the player using Gerber makes any bid other than 5♣, that is to play (including 4NT).

King Asking:

1NT — 4♣ — any — 5♣

  • 5♦: 0 or 3 kings

  • 5♥: 1 or 4 kings

  • 5♠: 2 kings, bad hand

  • 5NT: 2 kings, bad hand
