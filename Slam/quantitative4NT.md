### Quantitative 4NT
When suit agreement has not been reached, a bid of 4NT is quantitative (slam invitational).
There are a number of ways to accept the invitation.

4NT quantitative:
   * 5♣: 0 or 4 aces
   * 5♦: 1 ace
   * 5♥: 2 aces
   * 5♠: 3 aces
   * 5NT: pick a slam
   * any 6-bid: suggested place to play (pass or correct)
