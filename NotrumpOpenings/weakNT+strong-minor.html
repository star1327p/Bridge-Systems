Balanced hands have either 4-3-3-3, 4-4-3-2, or 5-3-3-2 distribution. This includes five-card majors unless the major is particularly strong. Semi-balanced hands with either 5-4-2-2 or 6-3-2-2 distribution may be treated as balanced if the doubletons are Qx or better.

A hand may be upgraded 1 point for a long suit with good texture (e.g., QJT9x) and downgraded 1 point for a 4-3-3-3 hand with poor texture (e.g., AJx-KJx-Axx-Qxxx).

The bidding structure for balanced hands is as follows:

  • 12-14: Open 1NT

  • 15-17: Open 1 of a suit, rebid NT

  • 18-21: Open strong, rebid NT

  • 22+: Open strong, jump NT
