Minor Suit Transfer Bids

Transfers are used to show hands with a long minor.

  • 2♠: transfer to clubs

    • 2NT: preacceptance (good support)

    • 3♣: normal response

  • 2NT: transfer to diamonds

    • 3♣: preacceptance (good support)

    • 3♦: normal response

After the transfer, responder can bid again to show extra length or strength: * New suit: shortness, mild slam try * 3NT: balanced, mild slam try * Raise to 4: invitational * Raise to 5: sign-off

When using this treatment, responder bids 2♣ (Stayman) with any game invitational hand. A rebid of 2NT over a 2♦ or 2♠ response shows an invitational hand that may or may not have a four-card major.
