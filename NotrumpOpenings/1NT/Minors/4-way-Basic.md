#### Minor Suit Transfer Bids

Transfers are used to show hands with a long minor.

   * 2♠ = transfer to clubs
      * 2NT: preacceptance (good support)
      * 3♣: normal response
   * 3♣ = transfer to diamonds
      * 3♦: normal response
      * 3NT: game is reachable opposite nothing more than Qxxxxx.
