2NT Minors

2NT shows an intermediate hand (generally 10-14 HCP) with at least five diamonds and at least four clubs. (The hands must either be equal in length or have longer diamonds.) It is forcing. All responses except 4NT are natural and non-forcing though opener can bid again with extra length.

  • 3♣, 3♦: preference

  • 3♥, 3♠, 3NT, 4♥, 4♠: natural, non-forcing

  • 4NT: Ace-asking (normal Blackwood)
