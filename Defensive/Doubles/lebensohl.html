Lebensohl over a Weak-Two

After a Weak-Two by the opponents followed by a Takeout Double and a pass by responder, 2NT is a conventional response, requesting (but not insisting) on a relay to 3♣. This allows Advancer to distinguish between minimum, intermediate, and maximum hands.

(2♠)X(p): * 2NT: Relay to 3♣ pass, 3♦, 3♥: to play (7 or fewer HCP) 3♠: 4 hearts, spade stopper, GF 3NT: to play, no heart suit, stopper The 3♣ Relay may be bypassed with 22+ HCP or 9+ playing tricks * 3♣, 3♦, 3♥: semi-positive (8-11 HCP) * 3♠: game values, 4 hearts, no stopper * 3NT: game values, no heart suit, no stopper
