﻿A 1♦ opener suggests a four-card or longer suit, since 1♣ is preferred on hands where a three-card minor suit must be opened. The exception is a hand with 4=4=3=2 shape which should be opened 1♦. With 9+ cards in the minors, generally open the longer minor first. If the minor is clubs, make sure you have a suitable rebid after partner’s response.
