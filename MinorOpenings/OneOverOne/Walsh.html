A 1/1 bid shows 6+ dummy points and either a 5+ card suit or no lower 4-card suit. Bid 4-card suits up the line but with two 5+-card suits bid the highest first.

1♦

A 1♦ response to 1♣ shows one of the following hand types:

  • Rebiddable diamonds

  • A game force with at least one 4-card major

  • A balanced 6-8 points and a desire for opener to declare 1NT.
