An opening bid of 2♦ shows exactly 4 spades and either 5 or 6 hearts with 10-15HCP. Responder is expected to pick a place to play or bid 2NT to inquire, but may pass with a poor fit and diamonds.

2♦:

  • pass or any minor: to play

  • 2♥, 2♠: to play (preference)

  • 2NT: forcing inquiry

    • 3m: 3-card minor (4=5-3-1)

    • 3♥: 4=5=2=2 minimum

    • 3♠: 4=5=2=2 maximum, bad minors

    • 3NT: 4=5=2=2 maximum, good minors

    • 4m: 4-card minor (4=5=4-0)

    • 4♥: 6-card suit

  • 3♥, 3♠: invitational

  • 3NT, 4♥, 4♠: to play
